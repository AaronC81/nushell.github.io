Delay for a specified amount of time

Syntax: sleep <time> [additional_time]...

# Flags

-h, --help Display help message.

# Examples

Sleep for 3 seconds

> sleep 3sec

Sleep for 1 minute and 2 seconds

> sleep 1sec 1min 1sec
